I am a PhD student in Computer Science at [Nanjing University](https://www.nju.edu.cn/), advised by [Prof. Bing Mao](https://seclab.nju.edu.cn/people/faculty/maob/).

My research focuses on **binary analysis** and **compiler chain modification** for customized security requirements, including reverse engineering, binary defense, and applying LLMs to firmware security analysis.

## News

- **[2023]** Paper OCFI accepted by **ISSTA 2023**.
- **[2022]** Paper on binary disassembly ground truth accepted by **USENIX Security 2022**.

## Research Interests

- Binary analysis and reverse engineering
- Compiler security and binary defense
