Research
======
My research focuses on innovative approaches to binary analysis and compiler chain modification for customized security requirements. Specifically, my work spans reverse engineering (ISSTA'23, USENIX Security'22, TOSEM) and binary defense (submitted to ISSTA'26). At Purdue University, I focus on applying LLMs to binary security, including neural-symbolic vulnerability analysis for firmware binaries.
